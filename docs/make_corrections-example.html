Post title What My Son's Results Reminded Me Of
My son recently learned that he didn't pass the October entrance exam for a college he applied to.
Because the school is notoriously hard to get into, the result didn't surprise me, though he was clearly disappointed.
Still, he has other chances coming up in January and February.
The situation reminded me of something a teacher told me back when I was preparing for university: that if I entered a university, I should carry myself as if I intended to put down roots there for the rest of my life.
Only now do I truly understand what that meant.
With that mindset, being part of the university comes naturally.
I hope my son ends up at a school where he can feel the same way.
